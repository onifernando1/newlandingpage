Shriti Fernando

Shriti Fernando is a contemporary artist based in London. She has exhibited in a variety of galleries, and is now accepting commissions. She is a self trained artist, who has been selling professional art pieces for over 30 years. She is well versed in a variety of techniques, from oil and acrylic paintings, to pen and ink drawings.
